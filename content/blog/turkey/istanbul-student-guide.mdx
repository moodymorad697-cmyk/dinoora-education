## English Content

### Complete Guide to Student Life in Istanbul 2024-2025

Istanbul, the magnificent bridge between Europe and Asia, is one of the world's most captivating cities for international students. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about studying and living in Istanbul.

---

## Why Choose Istanbul?

### 🎓 Top Universities

- **Boğaziçi University** - Turkey's #1, founded 1863. [boun.edu.tr](https://www.boun.edu.tr)
- **Istanbul Technical University** - Leading engineering. [itu.edu.tr](https://www.itu.edu.tr)
- **Istanbul University** - Oldest university, founded 1453. [istanbul.edu.tr](https://www.istanbul.edu.tr)
- **Sabancı University** - Modern research university. [sabanciuniv.edu](https://www.sabanciuniv.edu)
- **Koç University** - Premier private university. [ku.edu.tr](https://www.ku.edu.tr)

### 💰 Affordable Living

- Tuition: $2,000 - $8,000/year (public universities)
- Living costs: $350 - $650/month
- Türkiye Bursları scholarship available

---

## Transportation

- **Public Transport**: Metro, tram, bus, ferry. Get [Istanbulkart](https://www.istanbulkart.istanbul)
- **Ride-hailing**: [BiTaksi](https://www.bitaksi.com), [Uber](https://www.uber.com)
- **Biking**: [Bisim](https://www.ibb.istanbul) bike-sharing

---

## Food & Dining

- **Street Food**: $1 - $3 per dish. Try Döner Kebab, Simit, Balık Ekmek
- **Halal Food**: Available everywhere (Turkey is Muslim-majority)
- **Popular Areas**: Kadıköy, Beşiktaş, Eminönü

---

## Must-Visit Sites

### Historical
- **Hagia Sophia** - Byzantine masterpiece, now mosque. Free. [muze.gov.tr](https://muze.gov.tr)
- **Blue Mosque** - Stunning Ottoman architecture. Free. [bluemosqueistanbul.com](https://www.bluemosqueistanbul.com)
- **Topkapi Palace** - Ottoman imperial palace. $15. [muze.gov.tr](https://muze.gov.tr)
- **Grand Bazaar** - Historic covered market. Free. [grandbazaaristanbul.org](https://www.grandbazaaristanbul.org)

### Modern
- **Galata Tower** - Panoramic views. $6. [galatatower.net](https://www.galatatower.net)
- **İstiklal Avenue** - Pedestrian shopping street. Free

---

## Healthcare

- **University Clinics**: Affordable/free for students
- **Private Hospitals**: [American Hospital](https://www.americanhospital.com.tr), [Acıbadem](https://www.acibadem.com.tr)
- **Health Insurance**: Required ($80 - $150/year)

---

## Visa & Residence

- **Student Visa**: Required, apply with acceptance letter
- **Residence Permit**: Must apply within 1 month of arrival
- **Renewal**: Required annually

---

## Top 10 Tips

1. Get Istanbulkart for transport
2. Learn basic Turkish (50+ words)
3. Try Turkish food: Döner, Baklava, Turkish tea
4. Join university clubs
5. Explore historical sites weekly
6. Budget wisely
7. Stay safe in tourist areas
8. Network with professionals
9. Use public transport
10. Embrace the culture!

## المواصلات

- **النقل العام**: مترو، ترام، حافلة، عبارات. احصل على [Istanbulkart](https://www.istanbulkart.istanbul)
- **استدعاء السيارات**: [BiTaksi](https://www.bitaksi.com)، [Uber](https://www.uber.com)
- **الدراجات**: [Bisim](https://www.ibb.istanbul)

---

## الطعام

- **طعام الشارع**: 1 - 3 دولار. جرّب Döner Kebab، Simit، Balık Ekmek
- **الطعام الحلال**: متوفر في كل مكان
- **مناطق شائعة**: Kadıköy، Beşiktaş، Eminönü
